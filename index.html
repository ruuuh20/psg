Rennes - Paris Highlights

Introduced in the 78th minute against Rennes, Paris Saint-Germain striker Eric Maxim Choupo-Moting needed just five minutes to score his first Ligue 1 goal. Only two players have scored quicker debut goals with the capital club: Jay-Jay Okocha after one minute and seven seconds against Bordeaux (1-3, on 08/08/1998) and Christophe Landrin after 3 minutes vs Metz (4-1, 29/07/2005). Against Rennes, Paris Saint-Germain recorded their sixth consecutive win in as many Ligue 1 matches this term. It is the second time the capital club has achieved the feat, after last season... A win over Reims, this Wednesday 26 September at 21:00 CET at the Parc des Princes, will see Paris set a new club record. Last season, the club was held to a scoreless draw away to Montpellier in Week 7 (0-0, 23/09/2017)

After his equaliser just before half-time, Angel Di Maria is the only player to be directly involved in a goal in each of the six Ligue 1 matches this season (an assist in each of the first three matches and a goal in each of the next three). The Argentina international scored his 11th Ligue 1 goal from outside the penalty box since signing for Paris in 2015. No other player has done better over the same period.
